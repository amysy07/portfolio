Patagonia Logo Redesign

Competencies
  • Vector Illustration
  • Typography
  • Iconography
  • Illustrator
the challenge

Paying tribute to the past while refreshing the present

Patagonia boasts a legacy spanning several decades. With a loyal customer base and a reputation for quality, my goal was to rejuvenate their brand image while honoring their esteemed history.

the solution

Hidden details that tell a deeper story

The primary objective was twofold: first, to conduct comprehensive research into the company's origins, evolution, and core values, and second, to craft a modernized logo that encapsulates its heritage while projecting a fresh identity.

Research

In crafting the new logo, I dug into the brand's past, studying its history and the founder's story.

This research helped me understand the brand's values and find inspiration for the design. I started by writing down words that came to mind when thinking of Patagonia, and looking for any connections.

I delved deep into the past of Patagonia's founder, Yvon Choinard, and all of his amazing feats and accomplishments. I also researched some of the native flora and fauna of Patagonia, trying to find a way to tie them to the brand in a symbolic way.

Sketching

With the information I learned, I started sketching and experimented with positive and negative figures within the shapes I was building.
