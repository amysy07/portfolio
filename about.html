From biology and conservation to artistic adventures: a cat-loving girl's creative leap into design.

I'm Amy, a graphic design student!

My journey began with a deep love for nature and a career dedicated to preserving it. Now, I'm taking my personal passions and finding a space for it in design.

My work reflects the harmony between the natural world and artistic expression, resulting in designs that are both visually stunning and meaningful.

From eco-friendly branding to educational illustrations, I aim to create visuals that inspire and inform. Let's collaborate and bring your vision to life with a touch of nature's elegance!
