From biology and conservation to artistic adventures: a cat-loving girl's creative leap into design.

I'm Amy, a graphic design student!

After earning my bachelor's degree in biology from the University of California, San Diego, I explored various roles, from public health to conservation, before finding my true calling in design. My journey led me back to school at Kapi'olani Community College, where I'm gaining skills in typography, web design, and graphic design.

Outside of work, you can find me surfing, taking yoga classes, watching anime, cooking, or spending time with my partner and our two kitty fur babies, Kimchi and Plum. I'm committed to crafting designs that are both visually compelling and meaningful, always seeking to push creative boundaries.

If my work or story speaks to you, let's connect and collaborate!
